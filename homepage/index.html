We know, by now, that global economies have been affected by the COVID-19 pandemic. While speculations are rife on the subject, the reality is that there are Nigerians who subsist primarily on the informal economy who have become economically destabilized by the lockdown. Angels Among Us is a way for us to play our individual parts in helping these people.
